About Me

I'm a final-year Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering undergraduate at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka, maintaining a CGPA of 3.63/4.00. I've always been fascinated by how invisible signals bring the world together — a curiosity that naturally drew me toward RF and Microwave Engineering, RFIC design, Antenna Design, and Embedded Systems.

I previously worked as an Embedded Systems Engineering Intern at Paraqum Technologies (Pvt) Ltd, where I contributed to developing advanced RF and embedded solutions. Later, I worked as a Part-Time Electronic Engineer at Sentranova (Pvt) Ltd from March 2025 to September 2025, gaining valuable hands-on experience in hardware design, system integration, and real-world product development.

Currently, I'm focused on my final-year project and several ongoing research and design ventures that push my understanding of RF/microwave circuits, IC layout, and wireless communication systems. I'm passionate about turning complex engineering challenges into elegant, high-performance solutions that blend theory, design, and innovation.
