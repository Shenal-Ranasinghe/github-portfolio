Team Selection

Our team has been selected for the UNIC CASS 2026 IC Design Tapeout Program!

We are proud to announce that our team has been selected to participate in the UNIC CASS 2026 IC Design Tapeout Program. This is a prestigious opportunity to work on a complete chip design project using the IHP Process Design Kit (PDK).

Program Overview

The UNIC CASS IC Design Tapeout Program provides hands-on experience with the complete integrated circuit design flow, from specification to tapeout. This project will utilize the IHP PDK for chip design and fabrication.
